1. The service

Provider builds and runs a personalized AI outreach system for Client's sales outreach. The system:

  • Finds the right targets across Client's call points (FQHCs, addiction/MAT centers, behavioral health, and pain clinics) in Client's territory.
  • Surfaces the decision makers at each facility and pulls verified direct contacts so outreach reaches the right person, not a general inbox.
  • Writes personalized outreach in Client's voice, leading with the oral-swab story (dignity, real-time accuracy, cannot be cheated), with proven, calibrated questions built into the messaging.
  • Connects to Client's email to draft messages and replies for Client's approval. Nothing is ever sent without Client's approval.
  • Runs follow-up sequences and provides an outreach report showing what is working.
