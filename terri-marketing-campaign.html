Part 2

The funnel: how it reaches your website and courses

This is the key part. Social platforms create attention, they do not sell for you. The funnel's job is to move that attention off the platform, into one place we control, and turn it into an email we can follow up with.

  1. Attention. Quote cards and clips get seen and shared in your niche (faith, resilience, women who have been through it). Pure reach.
  2. The bridge to your site. Every post points people to one landing page. On Instagram and TikTok that is the "link in bio." Pinterest is special, every pin links directly to a web page, so Pinterest sends people straight to your book, workshop, or landing page.
  3. The capture. On that landing page we do not sell the course to a stranger. We offer something free, a free chapter, a reflection guide, or a seat at your free workshop, in exchange for their email. That email is the whole game: it is permission to reach them again.
  4. The conversion. An automated email sequence nurtures them, more of your story, more value, an invite to your live workshop, and then walks them up your ladder: journal, book, course, premium. People buy after they trust you, and email plus your workshop is where that trust is built.
Many posts → one landing page → email captured → nurture + workshop → book + course
