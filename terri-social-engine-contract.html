1. What Provider will do

Provider builds and runs an author-brand content engine that turns Client's book, story, and course into a consistent content-and-traffic system. Included:

  • Content creation: branded quote cards (still) as the primary lane, plus a set number of motion pieces per month, created in Client's voice and style.
  • Posting and scheduling across Instagram, TikTok, and Pinterest (and Facebook or YouTube Shorts where it fits), using Client's existing GoHighLevel account or an agreed scheduler.

  • Tracking dashboard: a reporting view of reach, clicks, and sign-ups by platform.
  • Monthly management: Provider runs and optimizes the engine each month and reports on what is working.

2. Scope and limits (so the service stays efficient)

  • Cadence: up to [3-5] posts per week, the same content set repurposed across platforms (not custom, bespoke content per platform).
  • Motion cap: up to [4] motion pieces per month at this tier; additional motion is billed separately or draws on Client's own generation credits.

  • Not included at this tier: unlimited content, bespoke per-platform creative, paid-ad campaign management, or full done-for-you operation. Those are available as separate or higher-tier services.
